1. Service Description

FuelSkip provides a platform for users to pre-book fuel at participating fuel stations ("Bunks"). By using our service, you are purchasing a digital fuel pass that can be redeemed at the selected station.

2. Digital Fuel Bookings

Fuel bookings generated on this platform are valid only at the specific participating fuel stations listed in the application. A booking is considered complete once the payment is successful and a QR code is generated.

3. Redemption

To redeem your fuel booking, you must present the generated QR code to the authorized attendant at the participating bunk. The attendant will scan the code to initiate the fuel dispensing process.
