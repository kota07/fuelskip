Refunds & Cancellations

Last updated: December 30, 2025

1. Cancellation Policy

Vouchers can be cancelled before they are scanned/used at the fuel station. Once a voucher is marked as "USED" by the attendant, it cannot be cancelled.

2. Refund Policy

If a technical error occurs and the fuel is not dispensed but the payment is deducted, a full refund will be processed within 5-7 working days to the original payment method.

3. Shipping Policy

FuelSkip provides digital vouchers only. No physical shipping is involved. Vouchers are delivered instantly within the app upon successful payment.

4. How to Request a Refund

Please contact our support team at support@fuelskip.com with your Voucher ID to initiate a refund request.
