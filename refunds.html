Refunds & Cancellations

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

Fuel bookings can be cancelled and fully refunded as long as the generated QR code has NOT been scanned or used at the fuel station. Once a booking is marked as "REDEEMED" or "USED", it cannot be cancelled or refunded.

2. Refund Process

If a technical error occur during the dispensing process or if you choose to cancel an unused booking, a refund will be initiated. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method.

3. Refund Timeline

Once a refund is initiated, it typically takes 5-7 working days for the amount to reflect in your bank account, depending on your bank's processing time.

4. Digital Delivery

FuelSkip provides digital bookings only. No physical shipping is involved. Your digital fuel pass is delivered instantly within the app upon successful payment.

5. How to Request a Refund

For any refund-related queries, please contact our support team at support@fuelskip.com with your Booking ID.
